#1 Dinner

Japan

Champon

Japanese: Cham-pon (ちゃんぽん)

Champon is a seasonal dish that uses a combination of ingredients such as pork, seafood, and vegetables.
These are fried in lard before a soup made from chicken and pig bones is added to create a flavorful broth.
As a result, each serving of Champon is different and offers a unique experience that varies depending on where you eat it.

watch

Ingredients

Vegetables
- ⅛ Cabbage
- ½ Onion medium size
- ½ Green onion
- ¼ Carrot
- 100 g Bean sprouts
- 1 clove Garlic
- 5 g Ginger

Protein
- 4 Prawns
- Kamaboko (Japanese half-moon-shaped fishcake)
- Chikuwa (Japanese tube-shaped fishcake)
- Satsumaage (Japanese fried fishcake) *optional
- 50 g Pork belly / Bacon thinly sliced

Other
- 300 g Champon noodles / Hokkien noodles
- 2½ cups Water
- ½ cup Milk / Soy milk
- 2~3 tbsp Asian chicken stock powder
- 1 tbsp Oyster sauce
- Salt & Pepper to taste
- 2 tbsp Vegetable oil
- 1 Large pot Water for boiling the noodles

#2 Dinner

Japan

Gyudon

Japanese: Gyu-don (牛丼)

Gyudon is a mouth-watering dish consisting of beef served over rice (gyu = beef).
It is a quick meal that is often served in specific restaurants or Japanese fast-food chains such as Yoshinoya.

watch

Ingredients

- 1 lbs sliced beef, (ribeye recommended)
- 1½ yellow onion, (sliced)
- 1½ tbsp ginger, (sliced)
- 1 cup water
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- ¼ cup mirin
- 2 tbsp sugar
- ¾ tsp dashi powder

#3 Dinner

japan

Gyukatsu

Japanese: Gyu-ka-tsu (牛カツ)

This dish involves deep-fried breaded beef, which is served with a side of cabbage, barley rice, miso soup, potato salad, and pickles.
The preparation method results in a tender and crunchy texture, and the dish has a flavorful taste that's not too overwhelming.
Although it's not as commonly available as the pork version.

watch

Ingredients

Beef Cutlet
- 2 Beef Steaks 3cm/1.2" thick, 120g/4.2 oz per steak, beef round or sirloin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cake Flour or All Purpose Flour
- 1 Beaten Egg small-size
- Panko finely ground, Japanese breadcrumbs

Kombu Soy Sauce
- 100 ml Soy Sauce
- 1 tsp Sake
- 1 tsp Mirin
- 1 piece Dashi Kombu Seaweed 3x3cm/1.2"x1.2"

Sesame Miso Sauce
- 1 tbsp Toasted White Sesame Seeds
- 1 tbsp Miso
- 1 tbsp Mirin
- 1 tbsp Vinegar
- 1 tbsp Olive Oil

Side Vegetables
- Cabbage Leaves
- Broccoli Sprouts
- Cherry Tomatoes
- Parsley Leaves

Other
- Wasabi
- Frying Oil
