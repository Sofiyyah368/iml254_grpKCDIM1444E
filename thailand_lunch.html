#1 Lunch

Thailand

Mango Sticky Rice

Thailand: Khao niao mamuang (ข้าวเหนียวมะม่วง)

Thai Style Mango Sticky Rice is a traditional Thai dessert where steamed glutinous rice is served with sweet coconut milk sauce and fresh mangoes

watch

Ingredients

- 1 kg. Thai sticky rice
- 800 ml. coconut cream
- 150 g. sugar
- 1 tsp. salt
- mango

#2 Lunch

Thailand

Tom Yum Goong

Thailand: tom yum goong (ต้มยำกุ้งนำ้ข้น)

type of Thai hot and sour soup where the iconic flavours come from 3 herbs: lemongrass, galangal, and makrut (kaffir) lime leaves

watch

Ingredients

- 350g medium sized shrimp
- 4 cups water (or use 3 ½ cups unsalted chicken stock if not using shell on shrimp)
- 2 stalks lemongrass
- 10 pc galangal, cut into thin rounds
- 6 kaffir lime leaves
- Thai chilies
- ½ cup evaporated milk
- 3-4 cups oyster mushrooms
- ¼ cup Thai chili paste (nam prik pao)
- 3 Tbsp fish sauce
- ¼ cup lime juice
- A big handful of chopped sawtooth coriander (culantro) or cilantro

#3 Lunch

Thailand

Stir Fried Noodle

Thailand: pad thai (ผัดไทย)

stir-fried rice noodle dish commonly served as a street food in Thailand as part of the country's cuisine. It is typically made with rice noodles, shrimp, peanuts, a scrambled egg, and bean sprouts. The ingredients are fried in a wok.

watch

Ingredients

- Rice noodles
- eggs
- tofu
- juice palm
- sugar or sugarfish
- saucefresh
- shrimp
- sprouts garlic
- chili pepper
- lime
- peanuts
